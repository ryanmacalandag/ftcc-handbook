# Finance Committee

Welcome to the finance committee documentation page.

## What does the finance committee do?

The finance committee is responsible for all the financial aspects of the organisation which includes:

1. Keeps track of the financial transactions
2. Maintains the organisation's bank account
3. Receives money from organisation business including membership fees, donations, grants, merch sales, event registrations and others
4. Disburses money for organisational expenses
5. Works with other committees to tap potential access to grants from various funding sources
